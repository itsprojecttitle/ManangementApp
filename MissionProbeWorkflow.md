# Mission + Probe AI Workflow

Use `Mission Briefing.md` as the source of mission truth.

Use `ProbeSkill.md` as the diagnostic and questioning framework.

Scope:

- Not limited to media, marketing, or social communication
- Applies to people, teams, organisations, law, finance, health, technology, operations, learning, and cybernetic systems
- Pain points can be biological, psychological, social, legal, financial, technical, systemic, or environmental

Tutorial:

1. Write or paste the mission into `Mission Briefing.md` first.
2. Read the mission for missing fields: objective, target, pain point, leverage, move, timing, risk, and success condition.
3. Use `ProbeSkill.md` to identify the pressure points, friction, incentives, blockers, and execution gaps.
4. Ask only the questions needed to complete or sharpen the mission.
5. Write confirmed answers back into the mission brief.
6. Keep raw probe notes, speculation, and exploratory reasoning separate from the final brief.
7. When the brief is solid, produce a short probe plan and an immediate execution checklist.

If the mission brief is incomplete:

1. Ask only the missing questions.
2. Prioritize: objective, target, system, pain point, leverage, move, success condition, timing, risk, and next action.
3. Ask one focused question at a time when clarity is low.
4. Write only validated answers back into the mission brief.
5. Keep raw probe reasoning, hypotheses, and unverified notes separate from the mission brief.

Output standard:

- Clean updated mission brief
- Short probe plan
- Immediate execution checklist
- Open questions that still need user input
