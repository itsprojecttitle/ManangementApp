## (Analyzing Engagement Impact of Lower-Tier Artist Friend Reposts)

### Operation:: Action
Observe and analyze the impact on engagement when friends of lower-tier artists repost content featuring original artists.

### Systems
*   Engagement with High-Value Individuals (HVI)
*   Comment sections (including historical data)
*   Private messages
*   Group chats (if accessible)

### TARGET LAYERS:
*   **Layer 3: General World Interest / UK Community (Views, Gender, Region).**
    *   Objective: Gauge broad interest and demographic reach within the UK and globally.
*   **Layer 2: Actual Communities (Engaged Commenters & Mentions).**
    *   Objective: Identify active communities through comments and mentions.
*   **Layer 1: High-Value Individuals (HVI) - Original Artists.**
    *   Criteria: The artist whose content is being reposted.

### First Probe: Analysis
-   **Expectations (Short-term):** Some general engagement but nothing groundbreaking.
-   **Expectations (Long-term):** Aim to have at least 1 new following.
