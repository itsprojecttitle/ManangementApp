# Mission Brief: Operation Comphy - Artist Release Engagement

## (Showcasing Releases & Influencing Audience Reaction)

### Operation:: Action
Post cover art and song snippet to showcase a release date.

### Systems
*   Social Networking System (for general activity and engagement)
*   Painpoint System (to identify audience needs and struggles through interaction)
*   Emotional System (Curiosity, Envy, Greed, Jealousy, Lust - to observe/influence reactions regarding time, progress, and human capabilities)
*   Influence System (to measure personal impact without tribal reliance)

### TARGET LAYERS:
*   **Layer 3: General UK R&B Lovers.**
    *   Objective: Gauge broad interest from the general UK R&B loving audience, including views, gender, and regional demographics.
*   **Layer 2: R&B Communities (Engaged Commenters).**
    *   Objective: Identify active R&B communities based on those who comment under R&B videos and related content.
*   **Layer 1: High-Value Individuals (HVI).**
    *   Criteria: The artists being engaged with, potentially those who comment and mention others.
